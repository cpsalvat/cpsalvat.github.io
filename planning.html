Accommodations

The wedding venue is about 30 minutes by car from downtown Seattle. Depending on whether you're just in town for the wedding festivities or also want to explore the city, there are plenty of different places to stay.

Hampton Inn Southcenter

We have a block of rooms reserved at the Hampton Inn Southcenter which is inexpensive, close to the airport, 30 minutes by car from the wedding venue and a 20 minute drive from downtown Seattle. Click here to reserve a room using our block rate.



Airbnb

There are plenty of Airbnb options available both in the Seattle area and near the venue. Check out options in Seattle and near the venue.

Airport

If you're flying in, our airport is Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA).

If you're not renting a car and are planning to carpool to the ceremony, there is a free shuttle to the Hampton Inn Southcenter, or you can take the Link Light Rail if you're heading to downtown Seattle from the airport.
