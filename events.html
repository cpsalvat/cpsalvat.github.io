Exploring Seattle

We love living in Seattle, and one of the things we're most excited about is getting the opportunity to share our city with our friends and family! If you're going to be in the area for a while before or after the wedding, here are some of our favorite places!

Things to Do

Chihuly Garden and Glass

Museum showing beautiful glass work by Dale Chihuly. It's a little pricey, but totally beautiful.

Pike Place Market

Yes, it may be touristy, but it's also a lot of fun to visit! Lots of flowers and crafts and produce, plus guys who throw fish.

Volunteer Park

A beautiful park with a water tower containing fascinating history and great views of the city. The park also has several lovely gardens, a small greenhouse, and the Seattle Asian Art Museum.

Golden Gardens

One of Seattle's few beaches, with a gorgeous view of the Olympic Mountains! Great to visit around sunset.

Ballard Locks

Watching boats going through the locks is pretty cool. As an added bonus, there's a lovely little park and a fish ladder!

Seattle Farmers Markets

Lots of wonderful food and local crafts! Most are on Sundays, if you're looking for something to do the morning before the ceremony.

Theo Chocolate

Small but mighty bean-to-bar chocolate factory in Fremont! Take a factory tour (be sure to book ahead online), or just swing by to taste some amazing chocolate.

Museum of Flight

Seattle is home to Boeing, which means that we've got a little bit of a thing about airplanes. It will make you interested in airplanes even if you didn't think you were interested in airplanes.

Seattle Central Public Library

Really cool contemporary building - definitely worth checking out if you'll be downtown.
